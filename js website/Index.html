Kyoya Tategami

Kyoya Tategami is one of the most fierce and strongest competitors in the beyblade world

He wields the might Leone(Lion) and has taken down and faced many of the strongest oppenents ever known

Kyoya is known for his bravery and his never-back-down attitude, and this was shown in his fight against Ryuga(the strongest competitor at the time). He didnt back down or show any fear.

He is a "Friend" of Ginka Hagane as in he never really shows his emotions so one could not tell if he really cares or not.

All anyone will say is that Kyoya is an opponent to be feared and treated with respect.
