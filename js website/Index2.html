Kai

Kai is the elemental master of fire in the ninjago realm.

He is known for being cocky, hot-headed, and reckless and this often gets him into trouble or in some sort of dilemma

But as reckless as he may be his a very skilled fighter, and his mastery of his fire abilty has made him a very scary opponent to face.

His sister Nya is the water ninja and their chemistry has helped their team many times.

Kai is also a skilled blacksmith as that was his proffesion before being a ninja.
